ENDINGS

CRUSHED
[AUBREY ACT] ENDING 1/5
Follow the path the game lays out. Find OMORI in the forest and proceed onwards, at the final area let him tag you. (Route 1)
MIGRAIN
[AUBREY ACT] ENDING 2/5
The same as 1, except die to the spikes rather than OMORI himself in the chase (there is a hidden achievement for doing the chase perfectly as well) . (Route 1)
CONSTRAINT
[AUBREY ACT] ENDING 3/5
Go to the left after the bridge, find the pearl key inside Aubrey's house. Go backwards after meeting up with OMORI. Go all the way back to the start and through the gates with the key you found. (Route 2)
BELITTLEMENT
[AUBREY ACT] ENDING 4/5
Do not collect the pearl key.
Go back to the beginning after finding Omori. After dialogue at the gate return to the top room, there will be a raft, jump across. You will enter the lab and be given two prompts after a cutscene, choose to try and escape. You will have to complete several puzzles (CAPTCHA help Here), after this continue onwards through blackspace, you will reach the ending in the final room. (Route 3)
HALLUCINATORY
[AUBREY ACT] ENDING 5/5
The same instructions as 4, except in the lab, choose to look around, interact with Puzzy and follow the prompts. (Route 3)
SILENCED
[KEL ACT] ENDING 1/5
Route 1: Go to the top left path and jump down the hole, find shattered OMORI's quiz. (To get a secret boss, answer 'omosun' to shattered omori's questions 3 times in a row- use rally spam to cheese said boss). Continue onwards to the maze and follow OMORI'S path. Look through the lab interacting with all the books, escape
VENOMOUS
[KEL ACT] ENDING 2/5
Route 1: Follow the same instructions as ending 1, except, successfully pass shattered OMORI'S quiz, then complete Puzzy's puzzle, go down at the crossroads rather than up. After OMORI leaves you in neighbours house, go to sleep. Then follow the prompted actions.
REFRAINMENT
[KEL ACT] ENDING 3/5
Route 2: Go all the way down, feed the fish a watermelon and then meat from the storage container. Go into the dump and build electricals (using a lemon from the vending machine), follow OMORI through the maze, check on Aubrey's room in neighbours room.
DISILLUSIONED
[KEL ACT] ENDING 4/5
Route 2: Follow the path of ending 3, going to the fish, then get the shovel, through the maze, and into neighbours house. Once entering the cutscene where Mari welcomes you, choose to stay with mari- in the following scene choose the prompt to 'fight'.
SPOILED
[KEL ACT] ENDING 5/5
Route 2: The same instructions as 4 exactly, but select the prompt to flee.
REUNITED
[HERO ACT] ENDING 1/6
Route 1: Successfully build the boat within the time limit. (This can be quite difficult! Often stated that this is expected to be one of the last achieved endings! I'll provide a proper guide later, follow This for now)
BURNING CORPSE
[HERO ACT] ENDING 2/6
Route 2: Fail to build the raft in time and be chased by OMORI, survive against him by running away in combat. And then in the overworld.
SUBMERGED
[HERO ACT] ENDING 3/6
Route 3: Go down rather than tagging basil after the cutscene, follow the path laid out by mari until the end.
OVERPOWERED
[HERO ACT] ENDING 4/6
Route 1: Die to a random animal attack, and then surrender.
BATTERED
[HERO ACT] ENDING 5/6
Route 1: Successfully build the raft in time and escape the volcano with basil, walk through the forest, and enter into the game of hide and seek, fail to succeed- losing all three lives, and surrender.
CAPTURED
[HERO ACT] ENDING 6/6
Route 2: Fail to build the raft in time, get into the OMORI battle and chase, die, and then surrender to OMORI.

HERO - ROUTE BADGES

FLOWER BOY SAVIOR
Route 1: Defeat all the... Basil-copters?... and other enemies before they reach Basil.
FLOWER BOY TRAITOR
Route 1 OR 2: Keep trying to leave Basil without tagging him.
NIGHTVISION
Route 1: Never use the flash when in the northern area.
PERSERVANCE
ANY ROUTE: Select "CONTINUE" after A: Losing to a enemy B: to AUB.EYX C: EXORI (Except if you're following route 1)
VAPID FORFEITING
ANY ROUTE: Select "SURRENDER" after A: Losing to a enemy B: to AUB.EYX C: EXORI (Except if you're following route 1). Needed for endings 4, 5, or 6.
CLOSE CALL
ROUTE 1: Follow Ending 1 or 6 but leave the island at the last minute.
YOU'RE BACK, MARI
ANY ROUTE (?): Random encounter. Similar to "Unwelcome Surprise". You'll get after multiple playthroughs.
