OMORI.EXE

OMORI.exe is a parody based on the Creepypasta "Sonic.EXE," created by Mr. JC Hyena. Something has fallen over OMORI! Save three vessels from his rampaging demise or doom them to a terrible fate. So many souls to play with, so little time. Wouldn't you agree? This mod contains horror, blood, gore, and such.
